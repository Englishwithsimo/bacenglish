Exercise 7: Writing - Movie Review

Copy this paragraph in your notebook.

I love watching movies in my free time. My favourite film is "The Hills Have Eyes". It is a horror and thriller movie. It was directed by Alexandre Aja, who is a very famous French film director. The film was released in 2006.
"The Hills Have Eyes" revolves around the story of an American family who travel to San Francisco through the Mexican desert. Suddenly, their car breaks down and they find themselves in restricted area where US army used to test nuclear weapons. At first, they think that the place is uninhabited. However, the family is shocked when they realise that there are other people in the area. Not long after, the family is attacked by a group of cannibals who live in caves in the desert. After that, those cannibals start killing the family one by one. The scenes of the killing are gruesome.
I really like this film so much for many reasons. The film is very amusing and entertaining. Besides, I enjoy watching adventure, crime and thriller movies. In addition, I experience different feelings while watching the movie. For instance, through the movie, I feel happy, sad, thrilled, scared …Furthermore, there is always a moral to the story in the film.
