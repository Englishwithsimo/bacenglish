Exercise 10: Writing - Formal Letter

Copy this paragraph in your notebook.

Marrakesh, Morocco. December 27th, 2026.
Ministry of education – Department of Social Affairs.
Dear Sir or Madam.
I am writing this letter to apply for a grant in order to establish 'Shine Bright' association in Marrakesh.
Our project focuses on the social sector. The main goals of this project include: fighting illiteracy among women and children, and encouraging women to take part in politics.
To achieve these objectives, our association will carry out several activities. For example, we will buy school materials like tables, books, pens and school bags for women in literacy classes and children in schools. Besides, we will provide literacy classes to help women learn basic skills. In addition, we will organize campaigns to encourage women to take part in political life.
To implement this project, we will need materials such as tables, blackboards, pens and a vehicle for transportation.
This project also needs a budget that can be estimated at 300.000 dhs.
For more information, please contact us at: englishwithsimo@gmail.com
Yours Faithfully
