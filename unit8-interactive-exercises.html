Exercise 5: Writing - Writing a formal email (The problem of stray dogs)

Copy this email in your notebook.

Dear Sir / Madam

I hope this email finds you well. I’m writing this e-mail to ask for help regarding the problem of stray dogs in my area.
In my area, there are many stray dogs in the streets. They walk in groups in the neighborhood. Sometimes, they are very dangerous and aggressive. This makes it very dangerous for people to go out especially at night. Besides, children are scared to go out and play outside. As a result, they spend all their free time at home.
More than that, stray dogs make a lot of noise at night. Therefore, people can’t sleep at night. This situation affects people in my area. For example, people do not feel safe when they go out. Some children are scared to go to school. Moreover, the area is very dirty and not organized.
I would like to suggest some solutions to stop this problem. First, the council should build a shelter for these dogs. In addition, they should take dogs to the vet in order to control their number and keep them healthy.
I hope you will consider my suggestions.
Thank you for your time.

Sincerely Yours
